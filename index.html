Charm Residences

Experience the cozy life suitable for every family at SMDC Charm Residences. Located in the flourishing East Metro, this complete garden-inspired residential development opens a world of wonderful pleasure and ease with its own commercial areas and SMDC’s captivating five-star amenities; combining convenience and style truly works like a charm. Hotel-Like Lobbies Inspired by elegant boutique hotels, Charm Residences’ lobbies welcome it’s residents with a stylish and posh atmosphere. Soft blue sapphire highlights and accents on the furniture make the place appear very light and pleasant to its guests.

Site Development Plan

Phase 1 consists of buildings A, D and E, Phase 2 consists of buildings B and C while Phase 3 consists buildings F and G. All buildings are 10 story high.

Amenities

Charm Residences boasts of charming garden resort-inspired amenities that include breathtaking adult and kiddie pools located in the central amenity area.

Units Offered

Charm Residences offers 2 bedroom units. There are countless options for Charm’s 2 Bedroom configuration. Area may be transformed to a work area, study or entertainment room to be converted to bedroom in the future.
